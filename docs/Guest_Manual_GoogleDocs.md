## Placing Your Order

### Step-by-Step Ordering Process

#### Step 1: Select an Item
Tap or click on any item card to open the customization window.

#### Step 2: Customize Your Item
A popup window will appear with customization options:

**Quantity**
- Use the **-** and **+** buttons to adjust how many you want
- Default is 1 item
- Order as many as you like

**Modifiers (Optional)**
- Check any boxes for special options (e.g., "With Ice", "Extra Rum")
- Available modifiers depend on the item
- Some items may not have modifiers

**Special Instructions (Optional)**
- Add any custom notes (up to 200 characters)
- Examples: "Extra lime please", "No ice", "Well done"
- A character counter shows how many characters you have left

#### Step 3: Add to Cart
Once you've customized your item:
1. Review your selections
2. Click the **"Add to Cart"** button
3. The popup will close
4. A cart badge appears showing your item count
5. You'll see a brief confirmation message

#### Step 4: Continue Shopping or Checkout
You have two options:

**Option A: Add More Items** - Keep browsing and adding items to your cart

**Option B: Proceed to Checkout** - Click the cart icon in the header to view your cart

#### Step 5: Review Your Cart
When you click the cart icon, you'll see:
- All items you've added
- Each item's quantity, modifiers, and custom notes
- Individual item prices
- Running total at the bottom
- Option to remove items (trash can icon)

#### Step 6: Enter Delivery Information
Click the **"Checkout"** button to proceed. You'll need to provide:

**Your Name (Required)**
Enter your first and last name so we know who the order is for.

**Delivery Location (Required)**

**For Beach Drinks:** Select your beach location from the dropdown:
- Main Beach - Left/Center/Right
- Pool Area
- Cabanas 1, 2, or 3
- Other (specify in notes)

**For Room Service:** Enter your room number.

*Note: If you're logged in, you can choose between room delivery or beach delivery.*

**Order Notes (Optional)**
Add any special requests or instructions for your entire order.

#### Step 7: Submit Your Order
1. Review all information one final time
2. Make sure your location is correct
3. Click the **"Submit Order"** button
4. Wait for the confirmation screen

#### Step 8: Confirmation
You'll see a success message with:
- ‚úÖ Confirmation that your order was received
- Your unique order ID (save this for reference)
- Summary of what you ordered
- Estimated preparation time
- Where your order will be delivered

**üéâ That's it! Your order is now being prepared.**

Typical delivery times are 15-30 minutes depending on current order volume.

## Viewing Your Order History

**üìù Note:** Order history is only available for logged-in users. Guest orders are not saved to history.

### Accessing Your Order History

- **Method 1:** Click the **"My Orders"** button in the header (appears when you're logged in)
- **Method 2:** After logging in, you're automatically taken to your order history

### What You'll See

Your order history displays all orders you've placed during your stay, with the most recent orders shown first. Each order shows:

- **üìÖ Date & Time** - When you placed the order
- **üçΩÔ∏è Items Ordered** - Complete list with quantities, modifiers, and custom notes
- **üí∞ Total Amount** - The total price for that order
- **üìç Delivery Location** - Where the order was delivered
- **üìä Order Status** - Current status with color-coded badges:
  - **New** (Yellow)
  - **Preparing** (Blue)
  - **Ready** (Purple)
  - **Delivered** (Green)
- **üî¢ Order ID** - Unique reference number

### Understanding Order Status

- **New** - Your order has been received and is waiting to be confirmed by staff
- **Preparing** - Staff have confirmed your order and are actively preparing it
- **Ready** - Your order is ready and on its way to you
- **Delivered** - Your order has been delivered successfully

**üí° Tip:** See something you loved? Each order has a "Reorder This" button that lets you order the same items again with just one click!

## Reordering Your Favorites

**üéâ One-Click Reordering:** Love something you ordered? Reorder it instantly without having to browse the menu again!

### How to Reorder

1. **Go to your order history** by clicking "My Orders" in the header

2. **Find the order you want to repeat** and click the **"Reorder This"** button

3. **Confirm your selection** in the popup that appears, showing all items and the total

4. **You'll be redirected** to the ordering page with all items automatically added to your cart

5. **Review and modify** if needed, or proceed directly to checkout

6. **Submit your order** just like a normal order

### What Gets Reordered?

When you click "Reorder This", the system automatically adds to your cart:
- ‚úÖ All items from that order
- ‚úÖ Same quantities
- ‚úÖ Same modifiers (e.g., "With Ice", "Extra Rum")
- ‚úÖ Same custom notes for each item

**What does NOT get copied:**
- ‚ùå Order-level notes (you can add new ones at checkout)
- ‚ùå Previous delivery location (you'll choose location at checkout)

### Modifying a Reorder

You have complete flexibility after reordering:
- **Add more items:** Browse the menu and add additional items to your cart
- **Remove items:** Open your cart and remove any items you don't want
- **Change quantities:** Remove the item and add it again with the desired quantity
- **Proceed as-is:** If everything looks good, go straight to checkout

**üí° Perfect for Daily Favorites:** Found your perfect beach drink or go-to lunch order? Use the reorder feature to get it again tomorrow without any effort!

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need to create an account to order?
**A:** No, you can order as a guest. However, creating an account gives you access to order history and one-click reordering.

### Q: How long does delivery usually take?
**A:** Typical delivery times are 15-30 minutes depending on current order volume and what you ordered.

### Q: Can I cancel or modify my order after submitting?
**A:** Please contact staff immediately after placing your order if you need to make changes. Call the front desk or find a staff member.

### Q: What if an item I want shows as "unavailable"?
**A:** Unfortunately, unavailable items cannot be ordered at the moment. They may be out of stock. Check back later or choose an alternative.

### Q: Can I order both Beach Drinks and Room Service together?
**A:** Yes! You can add items from both menus to your cart.

### Q: How do I pay for my order?
**A:** All orders are charged to your room. Payment will be handled during checkout from the resort. No payment is required through the ordering system.

### Q: Can I add special dietary instructions?
**A:** Yes! Use the "Special Instructions" field to note any dietary restrictions, allergies, or preferences.

### Q: What if I'm at the beach but ordering Room Service?
**A:** If you're logged in, you can choose beach delivery and specify your beach location.

### Q: The ordering system won't load on my phone. What should I do?
**A:** Try these troubleshooting steps:
- Check your Wi-Fi connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Contact the front desk if issues persist

### Q: Is there a minimum order amount?
**A:** No, there is no minimum order. Order one drink or a full meal - we're happy to deliver!

### Q: What if I forget my account password?
**A:** You can always order as a guest. For password reset assistance, contact the front desk.

### Q: Are prices shown in the ordering system final?
**A:** Yes, all prices shown are final. The total you see at checkout is what will be charged to your room.
