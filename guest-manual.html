Placing Your Order

Step-by-Step Ordering Process

Step 1: Select an Item

Tap or click on any item card to open the customization window.

[SCREENSHOT: Item card with cursor hovering over it]
Description: An item card highlighted, ready to be clicked

Step 2: Customize Your Item

A popup window will appear with customization options:

Quantity

  • Use the - and + buttons to adjust how many you want
  • Default is 1 item
  • Order as many as you like

Modifiers (Optional)

  • Check any boxes for special options (e.g., "With Ice", "Extra Rum")
  • Available modifiers depend on the item
  • Some items may not have modifiers

Special Instructions (Optional)

  • Add any custom notes (up to 200 characters)
  • Examples: "Extra lime please", "No ice", "Well done"
  • A character counter shows how many characters you have left
[SCREENSHOT: Item customization modal showing quantity buttons, modifier checkboxes, and notes field]
Description: The popup window with all customization options clearly visible

Step 3: Add to Cart

Once you've customized your item:

  1. Review your selections
  2. Click the "Add to Cart" button
  3. The popup will close
  4. A small cart badge will appear (or update) in the header showing your item count
  5. You'll see a brief confirmation message

Step 4: Continue Shopping or Checkout

You have two options:

Option A: Add More Items

Keep browsing and adding items to your cart. Your cart will remember everything until you're ready to checkout.

Option B: Proceed to Checkout

Click the cart icon in the header to view your cart and proceed to checkout.

Step 5: Review Your Cart

When you click the cart icon, you'll see:

  • All items you've added
  • Each item's quantity, modifiers, and custom notes
  • Individual item prices
  • Running total at the bottom
  • Option to remove items (trash can icon)
[SCREENSHOT: Shopping cart view with multiple items, quantities, and total]
Description: The cart modal showing several items with remove buttons and a total price

Step 6: Enter Delivery Information

Click the "Checkout" button to proceed. You'll need to provide:

Your Name (Required)

Enter your first and last name so we know who the order is for.

Delivery Location (Required)

For Beach Drinks:

Select your beach location from the dropdown:

  • Main Beach - Left
  • Main Beach - Center
  • Main Beach - Right
  • Pool Area
  • Cabana 1, 2, or 3
  • Other (specify in notes)

For Room Service:

Enter your room number.

Note: If you're logged in, you can choose between room delivery or beach delivery.

Order Notes (Optional)

Add any special requests or instructions for your entire order.

[SCREENSHOT: Checkout form with name, location, and notes fields]
Description: The checkout page showing all required fields filled out

Step 7: Submit Your Order

  1. Review all information one final time
  2. Make sure your location is correct
  3. Click the "Submit Order" button
  4. Wait for the confirmation screen

Step 8: Confirmation

You'll see a success message with:

  • ‚úÖ Confirmation that your order was received
  • Your unique order ID (save this for reference)
  • Summary of what you ordered
  • Estimated preparation time
  • Where your order will be delivered
[SCREENSHOT: Order confirmation screen with success message and order details]
Description: Green checkmark with "Order Submitted!" message and order summary

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to create an account to order?

A: No, you can order as a guest. However, creating an account gives you access to order history and one-click reordering, making future orders much faster.

Q: How long does delivery usually take?

A: Typical delivery times are 15-30 minutes depending on current order volume and what you ordered. During busy times, it may take a bit longer.

Q: Can I cancel or modify my order after submitting?

A: Please contact staff immediately after placing your order if you need to make changes. If preparation has already started, changes may not be possible. Call the front desk or find a staff member.

Q: What if an item I want shows as "unavailable"?

A: Unfortunately, unavailable items cannot be ordered at the moment. They may be out of stock or temporarily unavailable. Check back later or choose an alternative item.

Q: Can I order both Beach Drinks and Room Service together?

A: Yes! You can add items from both menus to your cart. However, they will be processed as separate orders by our staff to ensure efficient delivery.

Q: How do I pay for my order?

A: All orders are charged to your room. Payment will be handled during checkout from the resort. No payment is required through the ordering system.

Q: Can I add special dietary instructions?

A: Yes! When customizing each item, use the "Special Instructions" field to note any dietary restrictions, allergies, or preferences (e.g., "Gluten-free", "No nuts", "Dairy allergy").

Q: What if I'm at the beach but ordering Room Service?

A: If you're logged in, you can choose beach delivery when ordering Room Service items. Just select "Beach Delivery" and specify your beach location instead of your room number.

Q: The ordering system won't load on my phone. What should I do?

A: Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Check your Wi-Fi connection
  • Refresh the page (pull down on mobile)
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Try a different browser
  • If issues persist, contact the front desk for assistance

Q: Is there a minimum order amount?

A: No, there is no minimum order. Whether you want one drink or a full meal, we're happy to deliver!

Q: Can I see the menu without ordering?

A: Absolutely! You can browse both the Beach Drinks and Room Service menus without creating an account or placing an order. Feel free to explore before deciding.

Q: What if I forget my account password?

A: You can always order as a guest if you forget your password. For password reset assistance, please contact the front desk. Don't worry - forgetting your password won't affect your ability to order!

Q: Are prices shown in the ordering system final?

A: Yes, all prices shown are final. The total you see at checkout is what will be charged to your room. There are no hidden fees or service charges.
